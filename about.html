About :-

WebnD(Web and Design) is the official web development and design society of IIT Bhubaneswar.The club is dedicated to building, maintaining, and innovating digital platforms that support the institute's academic, cultural, and technical ecosystem.

WebnD plays a key role in designing websites for college events, student bodies, and initiatives, ensuring clean design, smooth user experience , and reliable performance. Beyond development work, the club actively promotes learning by conducting workshops, hands-on sessions, and collaborative projects in web technologies and UI/UX design.

This society provides a platform for students to explore frontend and backend development, modern frameworks, creative design practices, and to some extent competitive programming also.Driven by innovation and creativity, WebnD aims to bridge technology and design to create impactful digital solutions for IIT Bhubaneswar.
